About Eagle Eye

Eagle Eye is a web application designed to help users navigate to their desired destinations with ease. By leveraging interactive maps and user-friendly interfaces, Eagle Eye provides accurate routing and location services. Whether you're planning a trip or just need directions, Eagle Eye is here to assist you every step of the way.

Developed by The Eagle, this project aims to combine technology and convenience to enhance your travel experience. We are committed to continuous improvement and welcome any feedback to make Eagle Eye even better.
Note the application is still in development. We are sorry for any inconvenience it may cause.

Usage

To use Eagle Eye, simply navigate to the Home page where you can input your destination in the provided form. You can either type in the address or choose a location directly on the map. Once you've set your destination, Eagle Eye will generate the best route for you to follow.

The recommended way to input your destination in the form is by typing the name of the place and add a comma followed by the country. For example: "Accra, Ghana".

Make sure to allow location access for a more personalized experience, as Eagle Eye can provide directions based on your current location. Enjoy seamless navigation with Eagle Eye!
